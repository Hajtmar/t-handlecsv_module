The handlecsv.lua is a ConTeXt module, that enables data merging for automatic document creation in ConTeXt. The most common case for automatic document creation is mail merge. For example documents  as office letters, envelopes or labels for those letters, return-receipts for all letters, lists of recipients of those letters, can be generated automatically from the same CSV data.  Module handlecsv.lua could be used to generating of budgets or invoices.
